Je Hoon Chae (pronounced J\(\breve{\text{e}} \cdot\)H\(\bar{\text{oo}}\)n) is a graduate student at UCLA, where he is concurrently pursuing a Ph.D. in Communication and an M.S. in Statistics. His current research interests are: (i) the durability of attitudinal change on politically salient issues, especially as shaped by factual information learning; (ii) the integration of AI to enhance cognitive accessibility to high-quality information among underrepresented populations; and (iii) methodological challenges in causal inference involving noisy or high-dimensional data (e.g., proxy variables or text-based measures).
